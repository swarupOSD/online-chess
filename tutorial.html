The Battlefield

A standard chessboard consists of an 8x8 grid of alternating light and dark squares (64 squares total).

  • Files (Files): Vertical columns labeled a through h from left to right.
  • Ranks (Ranks): Horizontal rows labeled 1 through 8 from bottom to top.
  • Coordinates: Every square has a unique coordinate (e.g. e4: Column e, Row 4).
  • White Side controls ranks 1 and 2, while Black Side controls ranks 7 and 8 on start.
